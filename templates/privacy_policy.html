1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use our website, services, tools, and automation features (collectively, the "Service").

Our Service integrates with third-party platforms to assist users with listing management, inventory synchronization, analytics, and automation. Use of those third-party platforms remains governed by their respective privacy policies and terms.

2. Information We Collect
A. Information You Provide Directly
  • Account information (name, email, username)
  • Platform credentials or API tokens (when voluntarily provided)
  • Inventory data, listing metadata, pricing, descriptions
  • Uploaded CSV files or structured data
  • Support messages and communications
Note: Credentials are stored encrypted and used only to perform actions explicitly authorized by the user.
B. Information Collected Automatically
  • IP address
  • Browser and device metadata
  • Usage logs and timestamps
  • Automation execution logs (non-content based)

No biometric data collection. No surveillance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data solely to:

  • Execute user-requested automation
  • Sync listings, prices, and inventory across platforms
  • Detect duplicate listings
  • Provide analytics and estimates
  • Maintain system integrity and security
  • Comply with legal obligations
We do not sell user data.
We do not train AI models on private user data unless explicitly disclosed and opted into.
4. Third-Party Platform Integrations

Our Service connects to third-party platforms at the direction and authorization of the user. When you connect a platform account, you authorize us to access and process data as permitted by that platform and your account permissions.

Important Clarifications:
  • Each platform retains independent control over its data
  • Each platform's privacy policy continues to apply
  • We do not modify, override, or reinterpret platform rules
  • We act only as a technical intermediary
You acknowledge that actions performed through automation are subject to the rules, limitations, and enforcement mechanisms of the connected platform.
5. Automation & User Responsibility

By enabling automation features, you acknowledge and agree that:

  • You are authorized to access the connected accounts
  • You understand the platform's terms of service
  • You accept responsibility for actions initiated through automation
  • Automation executes exactly as configured by you
We are not responsible for platform penalties resulting from user-configured automation.
5A. Integration Methods

The Service may interact with third-party platforms using one or more of the following methods:

A. API-Based Integrations

Where available, the Service uses official APIs provided by third-party platforms, subject to rate limits, permissions, and platform availability.

B. CSV-Based Imports & Exports

CSV tools rely on user-supplied data and do not establish persistent access to third-party accounts. Data accuracy is the user's responsibility.

C. Browser or Session-Based Automation

Certain features may simulate user actions through browser sessions or automation tools.

You acknowledge that:

  • Browser automation may be more sensitive to platform changes
  • Platform detection or blocking may occur
  • Availability and reliability are not guaranteed
The Service makes no representation that browser automation is supported or permitted by any third-party platform.
5B. Automation Throttling, Rate Limits & Safeguards

The Service may apply automated safeguards to manage system stability, platform compatibility, and risk exposure, including but not limited to:

  • Rate limits
  • Execution delays
  • Batch processing
  • Temporary pauses
  • Feature-specific throttles
  • Platform-specific caps
Throttles may be applied:
  • Automatically by the system
  • Dynamically based on platform behavior
  • Per user, account, platform, or tier
You acknowledge that:
  • Throttling may delay execution
  • Throttling may prevent certain actions
  • Throttles may change without notice
  • Throttling does not guarantee platform acceptance
The Service does not guarantee real-time execution or completion of automated actions.
Tier Notice: Free tiers may be subject to stricter automation throttles, reduced execution frequency, or delayed processing.
Advanced: Execution Behavior Details

Automation Execution Behavior

Automation execution is subject to internal throttling designed to:

  • Reduce platform detection risk
  • Prevent accidental rapid actions
  • Maintain system stability
  • Respect platform rate constraints

Execution timing may vary based on:

  • Platform type
  • Action frequency
  • Account history
  • System load
  • Feature tier

Automation is best-effort, not guaranteed.

6. Data Sharing

We may share data only with:

  • Connected platforms (as instructed by the user)

We never share credentials with other users or third parties.

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to provide the Service or comply with legal requirements.

Users can:
